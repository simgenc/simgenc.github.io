Hi!

My name is Simge, and I am a mental health researcher at Copenhagen University Hospital, with a multi-disciplinary background in experimental psychology and cognitive neuroscience. My research history ranges from digital interventions in sleep & dreams, developmental cognitive neuroscience and wearable technology, with hands-on experience in functional neuroimaging as well as psychophysiological and behavioural data.

Currently with my work at the Child and Adolescent Mental Health Centre, I get to dive deeper into my passion of understanding the brain's complexities at the intersection of neuroscience, psychology, and technology!
